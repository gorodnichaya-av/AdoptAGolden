Butter II

Butter II

6 years old

Howdy friends! My name is Butter. I’m a 6 year old golden girl and I weigh 59 lbs. I haven’t had the easiest life so far. Some people did not treat me kindly and so I am learning to trust again. But as you can see, I am a pretty girl and I’m also really sweet. I am what some people call ‘Velco’ because I like to stick close to my people. Since I like all the attention, I might like to be an only dog. I am potty and crate trained and not a big barker. What I really need is to find just the right people to love me and want me to live with them forever. If you think we would be a good match, let AGB know you want to meet me.
